A personal project exploring the fundamental principles of Bauhaus design through a series of typographic posters. Each poster focuses on a single element — color, grid, scale, or negative space — and pushes it to its extreme.

**Process:**  
All posters were built with CSS and HTML, rendered in the browser, and captured at high resolution. No design tools were used beyond the browser's DevTools.

**Takeaways:**  
- Constraints are creative fuel  
- Asymmetric balance is harder but more rewarding  
- Primary colors don't need blending
