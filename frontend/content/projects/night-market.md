An experimental food discovery app built for night market enthusiasts. Users can browse stalls by location, read community reviews, and save their favorite finds.

**Stack:** React Native, Supabase, Mapbox  
**Role:** Design & Frontend  
**Status:** Open source, ~200 stars on GitHub

The most interesting challenge was designing for low-light environments — the app is predominantly used after sunset, so the dark mode wasn't an afterthought, it was the starting point.
